Benchmarking and Enhancing VLMs for Physical World Understanding

Motivation

VLMs are good at general vision QA, but it remains unclear whether they can understand physical properties and physical phenomena in the real world 🤔. Understanding the physical world is very important for many applications such as robotics and embodied AI 🤖, so it is necessary to build a benchmark to see whether VLMs can really understand the physical world 🌍.

  • 2. Benchmarking performances of different VLMs
  • 3. Draw some observations and submit a paper to ICLR
