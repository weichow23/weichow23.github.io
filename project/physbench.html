Motivation

MLLMs are good at general vision QA, but it remains unclear whether they can understand physical properties and physical phenomena in the real world. Understanding the physical world is very important for many applications such as robotics and embodied AI, so it is necessary to build a benchmark to see whether MLLMs can really understand the physical world.
